In this study, we will ask you to complete a task of booking hotel online and answer the questions in the follow-up questionnaire. The whole process will last about 30 minutes. The website will log your operations and timestamps during this study. Please follow the procedures and finish each step carefully.

Purpose of this Study
The purpose of this study is to evaluate users' decisions, behaviors, and perceptions of different designs of user ratings when they are booking hotels online mainly base on users' feedback.
Please avoid completing only the necessary steps. Our website will record all your interactions and check the quality of your feedback to decide whether to pay you or not
Procedures
Participation in this study will consist of the following four phases.
1. You will read an introduction about the task of booking a hotel base on a hypothetical story (about 5min).
2. You will browse the 9 hotels provided by us and learn about them in detailby browsing each hotel's ratings and reviews (about 20min).
3. You will need to choose top 3 hotels from them that you want to stay at, rank them and explain the decision you make (about 5min).
4. You will be asked several additional questions after you submit your ranking result and reasons in the Phase 3 (about 5min).
Participant Requirement
Participants need to have experience on booking hotels online and are not familiar with the local hotels in London.
Data Collected
We will not ask for your real name and contact information.
We will collect your answers to every question in the study.
We will collect your operations (e.g. mouse moving and clicking) for analysis and time using for analysis and quality review. We may publish research reports that include your result but your data will be anonymous.
Voluntary Consent
Participation in this study is voluntary. You are free to withdraw or discontinue participation at any time.
Compensation & Costs
Each participant will be paid after successfully finishing the study and post-questioinnaire.
If you follow the steps to give us high quality feedback, an additional 10% bonus will be paid to you.
There will be no cost to you if you participate in this study.
